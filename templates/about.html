About DietGenie

Your Personalized Indian Diet Recommendation System

DietGenie is an intelligent system designed to provide customized diet plans tailored to your unique needs based on Indian regional cuisines.

Our Mission

To make healthy eating accessible and personalized for every Indian by combining traditional wisdom with modern nutritional science.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted nutrition platform that helps people achieve their health goals through culturally appropriate dietary guidance.

How It Works

1
Input Your Details

Provide basic information like age, weight, height, region, and dietary preferences.

2
Advanced Algorithm

Our system calculates your BMI and analyzes your nutritional needs based on scientific parameters.

3
Personalized Plan

Receive a 7-day customized meal plan with regional Indian dishes tailored to your profile.
